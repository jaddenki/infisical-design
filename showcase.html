Design system / v2.0

A binary surface, a hairline grid, and one charged accent.

High contrast, sharp corners, two type families. Volt is the only color that carries emphasis. This page exercises every primitive in the system on a single canvas.

Surfaces are #ffffff or #111111, never in between. Volt is reserved for primary-button hover fills, selection, highlight marks, and key callouts.

11/Motion & effects

Cipher reveals on scroll, pixel-dither on hover, nothing else.

Reveal on scroll

Mono labels scramble in from random glyphs at 70ms stagger, capped at 1500ms total runtime.

Hover on label

Secondary buttons and inline arrow links scramble through the same glyph set. Tighter timings keep the run under 250ms.

Dither paint-in

Highlighted words may paint themselves in via the same pixel grid the first time they scroll into view, then stay filled.

Durations press · 120ms fast · 160ms base · 200ms slow · 280ms
